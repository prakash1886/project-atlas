# Intent: UI/UX & Portal Screens (SYS-UI)

## 1. Goals
Provide a clean, responsive, and high-density visual workstation to coordinate the platform's active workflows.

## 2. Constraints
- Strict dark theme matching modern webguidance styles.
- Smooth canvas performance under force-directed graphs containing up to 5,000 nodes.

## 3. Success Criteria
- Expose five core screens (Dashboard, Graph Explorer, Research Workspace, Content Factory, and Analytics).
