# Intent: Trend Intelligence (SYS-TREND)

## 1. Goals
Identify rising and evergreen content opportunities before competitors do, generating a high-potential content queue.

## 2. Constraints
- Avoid API bans by implementing randomized backoff and caching policies for Google/Reddit APIs.
- Scale signal collection to support up to 500M+ trend records annually.

## 3. Success Criteria
- Identify and score topics like "Tendulkar vs McGrath" or "Stoicism and Resilience" with a final score above 85.
