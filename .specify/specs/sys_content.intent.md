# Intent: Content Factory (SYS-CONTENT)

## 1. Goals
Convert raw intelligence and fact sheets into multi-channel publishable media assets at scale while maintaining strict branding.

## 2. Constraints
- Strict Human-in-the-Loop approval: AI cannot publish directly to live platforms.
- Track usage of stock asset licenses to guarantee copyright compliance.

## 3. Success Criteria
- Produce a formatted 2500-word blog article and a 15-minute YouTube script on approved topics.
